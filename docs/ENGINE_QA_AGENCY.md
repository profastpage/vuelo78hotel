# Agency / Personal Brand QA

## Core Objective

Agency and personal-brand pages must feel human, commercial, trustworthy, and case-driven.

## Required Checks

- authority is visible early
- services and proof feel coherent
- testimonials/cases reinforce the offer
- contact path is always clear
- layout supports clarity more than self-expression
- mobile keeps personal tone without clutter

## Failure Examples

- too many decorative sections and weak trust
- case blocks without clear business outcome
- personality stronger than clarity
- final CTA weak or hidden
