# Brand Site QA

## Core Objective

Brand sites must feel polished, editorial enough to build trust, and structured enough to remain easy to scan.

## Required Checks

- brand story is understandable quickly
- typography feels intentional, not oversized or broken
- sections maintain rhythm and narrative flow
- media and copy stay balanced
- trust and clarity remain stronger than decoration
- headers, section titles, and cards remain aligned
- mobile preserves narrative order without visual collapse

## Failure Examples

- page feels like disconnected sections
- editorial typography breaks responsive layout
- too much empty space without purpose
- brand tone and layout style contradict each other
